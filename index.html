01

Research Snapshot

5 gaps identified

The Five Gaps

  • Zero paid traffic infrastructure. 3,000 students from word-of-mouth proves product quality, but it is the ceiling. No ad account, no retargeting, no lookalike audiences from the student list.
  • No English-language funnel. UGC creator market is global. An English lite version opens US/UK/AU where brands pay 3-5x Balkan rates.
  • Fragmented web presence. minniementor.com, ttb.academy, and the UGC Executive Academy landing live on three separate properties. No consolidated funnel.
  • Minimal social proof on sales page. 3,000 students is strong but the page shows minimal testimonials. For a visual-skill program, proof should be visual (portfolio transformations, video testimonials).
  • No post-purchase ascension. 4-month program ends. No alumni community, no advanced tier, no brand placement pipeline. The highest-value move is connecting graduates to brands.

Highest-Leverage Fix

Build paid traffic on the existing student list (lookalike audiences from 3K buyers). Launch an English-language lite funnel. Consolidate web properties. Add visual proof and a post-program brand placement network.
